Armenia Health Facilities

This map contains the density of health facilities in each administrative district in Armenia normalized for population. Click to get details on population, Facility Counts and the exact ratio outlined in the legend below.

Motivations for this stem from getting more understanding of medical facility access for more rural areas in Armenia outside of Yerevan. Data for health facilities is sourced via the Humanitarian data exchange, specifically from Mark Herringer's contribution.

The geojson outline are clearly misaligned with the current layout of the region. Making it accurate is a next step in improving the map. The population values were sourced from a report following the 2010 Armenia Census from the Statistical Committee of Armenia.
